Available Classes

Muay Thai

The Art of Eight Limbs

At DoBu, we offer Muay Thai classes for adults. Muay Thai, also known as the "Art of Eight Limbs," is a striking martial art that utilizes punches, kicks, elbows, and knee strikes. Learn this dynamic martial art with our experienced instructors and improve your striking techniques, cardio, and self-defense skills.

Open Mat/Personal Practice

Train at Your Own Pace

DoBu provides open mat sessions for personal practice, suitable for all ages. Whether you're into Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, Judo, or Karate, you can hone your skills during these open mat sessions. Our facilities are available for your practice and training, allowing you to work on techniques, spar, and improve your martial arts abilities.
